# NotebookLM — 4-Slide Comic-Book Superhero Aesthetic Prompt

**Use case**: Generate 4 hero slides to accompany the 4-minute pitch (`JUDGE_4MIN_SCRIPT.md`). Slides should look like comic-book pages — bold, kinetic, pictorial, but NOT cluttered. Each slide hits one act of the pitch.

**Source documents to upload first**:
1. `FINAL_SUBMIT/NOTEBOOKLM_CONTEXT_FOR_VIDEO.md` (full 15-section context)
2. `FINAL_SUBMIT/JUDGE_4MIN_SCRIPT.md` (the pitch script with timing)
3. `FINAL_SUBMIT/EXEC_SUMMARY_ONE_PAGE.md` (single-page numbers)

After uploading those three sources to NotebookLM, paste the prompt below to generate the 4 slides.

## How to use after generation

1. Upload the three context docs above to NotebookLM
2. Paste the prompt into the slide-generation interface
3. NotebookLM will produce 4 slide concepts with image descriptions
4. Use those concepts as a brief for an image generator (DALL-E 3 / Midjourney / Imagen) to produce final hero images
5. Combine in Keynote/Slides with the headline text overlaid
6. Synced playback: each slide stays up exactly 60 seconds during the 4-min pitch (Slide 1 = 0-60s, Slide 2 = 60-120s, Slide 3 = 120-180s, Slide 4 = 180-240s)
